üí° Real-World Applications and Impact

The IT Act plays a vital role in safeguarding digital infrastructure across sectors. In the banking industry, it mandates end-to-end encryption and secure customer authentication. In e-commerce, it ensures digital contracts and transactions are legally binding. In government services, it supports online citizen portals and digital documentation. The Act also lays the foundation for regulating platforms like WhatsApp, Google, Facebook, and YouTube, ensuring accountability in the digital space.

For example, if a company suffers a data breach and fails to protect user information, the IT Act allows affected users to seek compensation, and authorities can penalize the organization for negligence. Likewise, online harassment, fake social media profiles, and the spread of obscene content can be prosecuted under various sections of this Act.
